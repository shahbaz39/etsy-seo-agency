Etsy SEO Agency — Modern Aesthetic Landing Page Brief

Design and develop a modern, premium, and high-converting landing page for a brand called Etsy SEO Agency that helps Etsy sellers grow their traffic, rankings, and sales through professional SEO services.

The landing page should have a clean SaaS-style aesthetic, smooth animations, elegant spacing, modern typography, and a visually premium experience. The overall feel should be similar to top modern startup websites with a focus on trust, conversion, and professionalism.
